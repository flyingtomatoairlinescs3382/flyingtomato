Who We Are

Flying Tomato Airlines is more than just an airline

We at CityU Hong Kong dedicate our entire life to Flying Tomato Airlines. Our mission is to provide the best booking experience, the best prices and an unforgettable flight. Once you have booked your flights to your favourite destinations in Asia we will send you all the information you need to manage your booking. When you arrive at CityU Airport Hong Kong you will go to CS3382 Terminal. Once you have checked in with our flying tomato airlines staff you will proceed to the gate our the flying tomato airlines lounge. If you are a member of the flying tomato CS3382 club you can visit the CityU Lounge for free and enjoy our famous Tomato Juice. This fresh tomato juice is also offered to all passengers on our flights. Our Tomato juice is made of a mixture of Hong Kong, Japanese and Spanish Tomatoes. We mix it and add just a little CS3382. It is all produced in Hong Kong at CityU in the Flying Tomato Airlines department.
