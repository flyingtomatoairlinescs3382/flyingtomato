Flying Tomato Experiences

From CityU Hong Kong to Bangkok with Flying Tomato Airlines

I flew with Tomato Airlines from Hong Kong to Bangkok on the CS3382 flight in September 2016. It was a very easy and convenient booking procedure. I left CityU around two hours before the flight and the check in staff was very nice and helpful. The flight boarded on time and we where up in the air quite quickly. Flying tomato airlines also has the best tomato juice, which they actually produce fresh everyday near CityU in Hong Kong. When we landed we got a little Flying Tomato Keychain.

Mark Twain
